Accident and Emergency (A&E)
Operates 24 hours a day and are staffed and equipped to deal with all emergencies.
Cardiology
Provides medical care to patients who have problems with their heart or circulation.
Diagnostic Imaging
Formerly known as X-ray, this department provides a full range of diagnostic imaging services.
Endoscopy
Involves a small thin tube with a camera on the end which is guided down into the throat.
General Surgery
The general surgery ward covers a wide range of surgery and includes day surgery, thyroid surgery, etc.
Haematology
Haematology services work closely with the hospital laboratory. These doctors treat blood diseases and malignancies linked to the blood.
